   Detection at (seed,frame) depends on rng(seed+frame)
   Same rng state reproduced across different seeds -> clustering

2. P(consec 4 miss)=0.4^4=0.0256
   Expected 6 zones of >=4 in 250 rng states, observed 3
3. Gradient: larger seed -> bad frames earlier -> UKF weaker
4. This is NOT a bug, it is mathematical consequence of rng(seed+k)
5. Mitigations:
   (a) K_loss: 4->6 reduces bad zones by factor 6
   (b) rng(shuffle) breaks Toeplitz (but non-reproducible)
   (c) Dual-radar fusion: ~85% bad seeds saved
   (d) Re-init timeout: 6->3 frames
   (e) Long-term: IMM reduces K_loss sensitivity
